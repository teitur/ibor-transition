\subsection{Background}
The London Interbank Offered Rate (LIBOR) is currently produced in 7 tenors (overnight/spot next, one week, one month, two months, three months, six months and 12 months) across 5 currencies: USD, GBP, JPY, CHF and EUR. These fixings will be published until the end of 2021. By then all market participants must have removed dependencies on LIBOR.
